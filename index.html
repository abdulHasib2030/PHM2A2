BMI Calculator

Understanding your BMI helps set realistic fitness and weight goals tailored to your body type. Regularly tracking BMI provides insight into potential health risks, allowing for early intervention.

BMI Calculator Chart

BMI Weight Status
Below 18.5 Underweight
18.5 - 24.9 Healthy

Calculate your BMI

The BMI form is designed to calculate your body mass index, a number that helps assess whether you are underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese based on your height and weight. This can provide insight into your overall health and guide your fitness or dietary decisions.
